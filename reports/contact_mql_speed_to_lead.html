Metric Definitions
% Reached
% of MQL contacts who received at least one logged call or 1:1 sales email after their MQL date.
Median Biz Hrs to First Contact
Middle value of business hours elapsed between MQL timestamp and the first logged call or email. Business hours = Mon–Fri 9am–5pm CT only.
% <4h / % <24h
Share of contacts first reached within 4 business hours (half a business day) or 24 business hours (3 business days — since each day = 8 hrs).
Avg Acts (10d)
Average number of completed calls + 1:1 emails logged in the 10 business days following MQL date.
Avg Calls
Average completed/connected calls only (QUEUED, MISSED, CANCELED excluded).
Avg Emails
Average 1:1 outbound or inbound sales emails. Bulk marketing sends are excluded.
