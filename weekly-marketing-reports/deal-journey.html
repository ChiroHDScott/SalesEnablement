Average Pre-Deal Touches — NB vs EB

What is a "touch"? A touch is a tracked interaction with the deal's primary contact that occurred before the deal was created. If no activity of a given type occurred pre-deal, it counts as 0 for that deal.

Touch types counted:
• Meeting — a logged meeting (calendar event) with the contact
• Call — a logged phone call with the contact
• Sales Email — a tracked CRM/1:1 email (sent or received via HubSpot)
• Form Submission — a web form filled out by the contact
• Marketing Email Click — a unique marketing email that the contact clicked a link in. Same email clicked 10 times = 1 touch. Multiple people at the same clinic each clicking = 1 touch per person.

NOT counted: Email opens (unreliable due to pixel blocking), website visits (passive), sequence enrollments (internal action).
Note: Activity counts include total logged activity for contacts who had pre-deal engagement. For contacts with activity spanning pre- and post-deal, counts may include some post-deal activity.
